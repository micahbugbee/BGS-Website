Mudlogging Services

  • 24 hour coverage with a 2 man (12 hour shift) mudlogging unit. This ensures complete and total monitoring of the well in case of encountering any unforeseen geological circumstances at any time.

    • Or

  • Traditional one man, 24-hour extended coverage for mudlogging services. We remain on site 24 hours a day, while catching our own samples with the frequency depending on drill rate, upcoming zones of interest, and adjust as needed upon request by you.
  • Mudloggers will be responsible for catching and cleaning their own samples.
  • Daily mud logs, company man & geological reports sent to a provided distribution list.
  • Sample pictures included on mud log for each formation encountered in the vertical portion, and throughout the horizontal.
  • Daily correlations provided through the vertical & curve portion of well, and as needed for upcoming zones of interest.
  • We also provide a set of dry samples at the end of the well, or can mail to a lab of your choice.
  • Additional lab related services that can be provided at an additional expense: XRF sample analysis via Chemostrat, Mud Gas isotope and Headspace gas analysis via GeoMark.

Gas Analyzing Equipment

We utilize the state of the art Bloodhound brand natural gas detector and chromatograph system produced by I-Ball Instruments, based on patented infrared sensors that are much more reliable than hotwire and FID gas sensors. In addition to total gas, the Bloodhound measures chomat analysis of C1-NC5 (methane to normal pentane), as well as H2S and CO2. It is compatible for WITS with Pason and Totco computer rig systems. Also, as needed, we can supply you with a login and password to watch rig activity remotely from your phone or computer. For any additional information on I-Ball Instruments and the Bloodhound, feel free to check out their website: www.iballinst.com/bloodhound.
